Why I Play

Guitar isn't just a hobby, it's a discipline for me. When I learn to play it brings me patience, persistence, and the satisfaction of getting better each day.

How I Learned

I started with open chords, then barre chords, then scales. The F chord is where everyone struggles. Here's the order I'd recommend:

  1. Open chords — E, A, G, C, D
    • These get you through most campfire songs
    • F major is the first real wall you hit
  2. Barre chords — moveable shapes up the neck
    • Once you get these, every key opens up
    • Takes weeks of sore fingers
  3. Scales and lead playing
    • Pentatonic minor is the backbone of rock and blues
    • This is where it starts getting fun
